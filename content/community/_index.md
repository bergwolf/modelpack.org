## FAQ

### How can I propose a change to the specification?

Open an issue on GitHub describing your proposal. For major changes, start a discussion first to gather feedback.

### Who owns the ModelPack project?

ModelPack is a CNCF Sandbox project under the Linux Foundation. It is vendor-neutral and community-driven.

### Can my company adopt ModelPack?

Yes! ModelPack is designed to be a vendor-neutral standard. Companies can adopt it, contribute to it, and build products around it.

### How do I report a security issue?

Please report security issues responsibly by emailing the maintainers privately. See the [Security Policy](https://github.com/modelpack/model-spec/blob/main/SECURITY.md) for details.

### Can I use ModelPack in commercial products?

Yes! ModelPack is licensed under Apache 2.0, which allows commercial use.
